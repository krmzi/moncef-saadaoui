"HELLO."

I'm Moncef Saadaoui, a self-taught graphic designer & full-stack web developer born and based in Rabat, Morocco 🇲🇦. With over 4 years of hands-on experience, I blend visual creativity with technical precision to deliver outstanding digital products.

My journey started in 2020 with graphic design — logos, branding, social media — and quickly evolved into building robust web platforms. Today I run live projects like DFK Team (a manga reading site) and Krmozi Streaming, serving thousands of Arabic-speaking users.

I specialize in combining art with logic: beautiful interfaces powered by solid back-end systems. Whether it's Photoshop, Figma, PHP, Python, or React — I make it work together. Currently also teaching IT & Design at ESINT School, Rabat.
